ARTS 486 : Project 3

UI Design Process - Person, Place, Thing


For this project, I decided to make a website called "Nostalgic Eats: Chinese Bakeries".

I came up with this project because I was craving some sweets that reminded me of my childhood. Growing up, I lived in a predominately Asian community in Brooklyn where there were bakeries on every block. Also, my family traveled into Chinatown often to visit my grandparents and alas where we had tutoring, swim practices, and dreaded Chinese school to learn Mandarin. During lunch the quickest and cheapest places to eat or grab a snack were the Chinese bakeries around us.

In order to start this project, I created a style guide as well as a logo:



Next, I had to figure out what content I wanted to include in the website and how I would design the graphics. Since I wanted to emulate the nostalgic feeling, I used a colored pencil texture to my illustrations.

On the home page, I had photos from my favorite Chinese bakeries back at home in NYC and set it as a gif on the home page. My other two pages of content are Favorites and Catalog. The Favorites page has a few of my absolute favorite items of all time that everyone should try at least once if they find themselves in a Chinese bakery. The Catalog page is a gridded layout of graphics that I made with an image slider.
