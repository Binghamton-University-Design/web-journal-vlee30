Project 1:

A Visual Message for the Inhabitants of Other Celestial Bodies

For Project 1, we were challenged to create a plaque design to be mounted on a rocket sent into space. If other celestial bodies were to stumble upon this, what would they learn about humankind?

Coming up with the design and components that I wanted to showcase began with the idea of philosophy. Specifically, the philosophical statement by Ren√© Descartes of Cogito, ergo sum aka "I think, therefore I am". In my opinion, the human brain is one of the most complex and interesting things about us. Humans are thinking creatures with feelings, morals and values which makes us all unique individuals that are also connected in the bigger picture of humanity.

I wanted to represent this idea as a technical drawing. I designed zoomed in portions of the brain located within the skull that continues zooming in until the neurons and synapses.The visual hierarchy of my project is that similar to a pyramid. The largest portion as the skull at the top and each layer is a zoomed in portion of the previous layer. They are connected by lines to further communicated that idea.
